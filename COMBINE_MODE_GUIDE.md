# COMBINE MODE - A Dog's Journey to Become Human

## Story
You play as a dog who dreams of becoming human. By collecting human items scattered across the world, you gradually transform from dog to human!

### Controls
- **Arrow Keys** or **WASD**: Move your dog/character around
- **ESC**: Exit to main menu

### Transformation Stages

#### Stage 1: Dog (0-30%)
- "A Dog's Dream..."
- You appear as a brown dog with:
  - Furry body
  - Dog head with floppy ears
  - Wagging tail
- Sky is blue

#### Stage 2: Hybrid (30-70%)
- "Becoming More Human..."
- You're a dog-human hybrid:
  - More upright posture
  - Human-like body with clothes
  - Still has dog head and small ears
  - Arms instead of front paws
- Sky starts turning pink

#### Stage 3: Almost Human (70-99%)
- "Almost There!"
- You look mostly human:
  - Human head with hair
  - Proper human body with blue shirt
  - Arms and legs
  - Human skin tone
- Pink sky

#### Stage 4: Full Human (100%)
- "YOU ARE HUMAN!"
- Fully transformed human!
- Golden sparkles circle around you
- Achievement unlocked!
- Sky is pink/red
- Message: "You achieved your dream of becoming human!"

### Features
- **Dynamic transformation**: Your character's appearance changes gradually
- **Endless gameplay**: Items spawn continuously
- **Scrolling world**: Items move from right to left
- **Collision detection**: Walk into items to collect them
- **Progress tracking**: Transformation percentage shown on screen
- **Celebration effects**: Golden sparkles when you reach 100%

## How to Access
1. Complete the entire hack sequence
2. Get to Grass Mode (after wolf eats everything and vomits it up)
3. Type **ttt** in Grass Mode
4. Wolf vomits up "Combine Mode"
5. Select Combine Mode from menu

## Trivia
- The name "Combine Mode" refers to combining dog and human traits
- This mode represents the ultimate transformation - achieving your dreams!
- The items you collect represent different aspects of human life
