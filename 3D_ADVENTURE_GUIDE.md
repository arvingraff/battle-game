## ðŸ’¡ Tips & Tricks

### Combat Tips:
1. **Keep your distance** - Only get close when ready to attack
2. **Watch your health** - Red bar at top left
3. **Enemies counter-attack** - You take damage when you hit them
4. **Level up heals you** - Gain 100 EXP to level up (defeats give 20-30 EXP)
5. **Use the Health Potion** before the Dragon fight
6. **Better weapons help** - Golden Sword +10, Mystic Staff +15 damage

### Navigation Tips:
1. **Use the minimap** (M key) - Shows your position (yellow dot) and enemies (red dots)
2. **Open all doors** - Press E when facing brown doors
3. **Don't get lost** - Map shows walls (gray), floor (black), treasures (gold)
4. **Enemy detection** - You see a red combat panel when enemies are near

### Quest Tracking:
1. **Press Q** to see quest progress anytime
2. **Check quest log** to see what's left to do
3. **Green checkmarks** mean quest complete
4. **Portal only works** when ALL quests done
